Our Commitment

We aim to ensure that our platform is accessible to everyone. Our goal is that people with disabilities can access **, perceive, understand, navigate and interact with our content** (text, images, video), as well as contribute if needed.

Accessibility Features

  • Full keyboard navigation â€” users should be able to navigate without a mouse.
  • Semantic HTML elements and ARIA landmarks to help screen-reader users.
  • High contrast mode & scalable text for readability.
  • Alternative text for images and captions/transcripts for videos.
  • No content that flashes more than three times per second, to reduce risk of seizures.
